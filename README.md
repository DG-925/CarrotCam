## What is CarrotCam?

CarrotCam turns your Android phone into a high-quality webcam for your Windows PC. Connect via **USB** (fastest) or **WiFi** (no cables), and use your phone camera in Discord, Zoom, OBS, Teams, and any app that supports a webcam.

### Virtual Camera Driver

CarrotCam installs a **virtual camera driver** on your PC, so your phone shows up as a real webcam device. Apps see it just like any other camera — no special integration needed.

## Getting Started

1. **Install** the desktop app on your PC and the APK on your phone
2. **Open** CarrotCam on both devices (they must be on the same WiFi network for wireless)
3. **Select** USB or WiFi on the desktop app
4. **Connect** — your phone camera will appear as a webcam on your PC
5. **Use it** — open Discord, Zoom, OBS, or any video app and select "CarrotCam" as your camera

### USB Setup

USB mode requires [ADB (Android Debug Bridge)](https://developer.android.com/tools/adb). CarrotCam bundles ADB — just enable **USB Debugging** on your phone and connect via USB cable.

### WiFi Setup

WiFi mode works automatically — both devices just need to be on the same network. The desktop app will discover your phone automatically.
